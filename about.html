Our Story

`PETRA Ready Mixed Concrete stands as a pillar of reliability in the Saudi Arabian construction sector. Located strategically in Riyadh, our state-of-the-art factory is engineered to meet the massive demands of Riyadh’s 2026 infrastructure boom. We don’t just supply concrete; we provide the literal foundation for the Kingdom’s Vision 2030 projects. Our facility utilizes fully automated batching plants to ensure every cubic meter of concrete meets rigorous international and Saudi standards. From high-rise foundations to industrial flooring, PETRA Concrete is trusted by top-tier contractors for its consistency, strength, and logistical precision. Our dedicated fleet of transit mixers and high-pressure pumps ensures that your project remains on schedule, no matter the scale.`
