Privacy Policy

PETRA Ready Mixed Concrete is fully committed to safeguarding the confidentiality, integrity, and security of all business data entrusted to us by our clients and partners. We collect and process personal data — such as project specifications, contact information (names, phone numbers, emails, and company details), and other relevant details — exclusively for the purposes of preparing, executing, and managing contracts, delivering our ready-mixed concrete services, and fulfilling our contractual obligations. This data collection is strictly limited to what is necessary (data minimization principle) and is processed in a fair, lawful, and transparent manner. We do not collect excessive or irrelevant information, nor do we use the data for marketing, profiling, or any purposes unrelated to our agreed business relationship. All personal data is handled in full compliance with the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia's Personal Data Protection Law (PDPL) — Royal Decree No. M/19 dated 9/2/1443H (as amended) — and its Implementing Regulations. This includes adhering to key principles such as: Lawfulness, fairness, and transparency Purpose limitation (data is used only for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes) Data minimization Accuracy Storage limitation Integrity and confidentiality (through appropriate technical and organizational security measures) We implement robust administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or disclosure. Access to data is restricted to authorized personnel only, and we ensure that any third-party processors (if involved) comply with the same high standards. In line with the PDPL, clients have rights including the right to access, correct, or request deletion of their personal data (subject to contractual and legal obligations), and we maintain records of our processing activities as required. PETRA Ready Mixed Concrete takes its responsibility to protect client data very seriously. We strive to build and maintain trust through responsible data practices that align with both our business values and the applicable laws of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. For any questions regarding our data protection practices or to exercise your rights under the PDPL, please contact our designated representative at +966 53 785 0627.
